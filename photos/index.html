Jimmy Feng's Photography

Photography is geographic field work and an artistic medium in which I depict my understanding of earth’s natural beauty and the interplay between humans and their environments. As Carl Sauer details in his Outline for Fieldwork in Geography, one should always “carry a camera… [and]… use much care in composition and exposure; views are of as much importance as notes” (Jones and Sauer 1915, 522). Shown are select photographs I have taken throughout my travels.
